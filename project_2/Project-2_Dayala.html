Every day, we make millions of decisions.


Most of them are fast and basic:
Who is that? Am I safe? Can I eat this?

In order to do that, we break things down into simple ideas and symbols.
For example, we divide the natural world into “good” and “bad”.

We look up to the sky
and think to ourselves...


BIRD?
GOOD
OR BAD?
How do you decide?
